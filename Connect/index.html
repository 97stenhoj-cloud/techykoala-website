Frequently Asked Questions

Is Connect really free?

Yes! Connect is free forever with 3 categories per mode, 30 questions each. Upgrade to Premium for 24+ categories, 75 questions each, and no ads.

What's the difference between modes?

Each mode is designed for different relationships: Couple (romantic), Friends (social fun), Family (all ages), Personal (custom decks), and Pandora (multiplayer).

How does Pandora Mode work?

The host creates a session and shares a PIN. Everyone joins, submits questions, and the host controls the game. Questions are deleted after the session for privacy.

Can I try premium categories first?

Yes! Every premium category offers a 5-question preview so you can try before upgrading.

What languages do you support?

Connect is available in English, Danish, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Romanian, and French.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime - no questions asked.
