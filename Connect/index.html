Frequently Asked Questions

Is Connect really free?

Yes! Connect is free forever with 3 categories per mode, 30 questions each. Choose from Basic (‚Ç¨6.99/mo), Premium (‚Ç¨11.99/mo with Spark Mode), or Premium+ (‚Ç¨19.99/mo with more Spark questions) for the full experience.

What's the difference between modes?

Each mode is designed for different relationships: Couple (romantic), Friends (social fun), Family (all ages), Personal (custom decks), Pandora (multiplayer), and Spark (AI-generated questions).

How does Pandora Mode work?

The host creates a session and shares a PIN. Everyone joins, submits questions, and the host controls the game. Questions are deleted after the session for privacy.

What is Spark Mode?

Spark Mode uses AI to generate custom questions tailored to your preferences. Set Energy, Depth, and Spice levels, choose your occasion, and get unique questions instantly. Premium users get 150 questions/month, Premium+ users get 400/month. Questions expire after 24 hours but can be saved to favorites.

Can I try premium categories first?

Yes! Every premium category offers a 5-question preview so you can try before upgrading.

What languages do you support?

Connect is available in English, Danish, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Romanian, and French.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play. Cancel anytime - no questions asked.
