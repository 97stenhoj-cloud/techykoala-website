Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Connect ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Connect mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (when you sign up)
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile information from Google/Apple sign-in

Usage Data

  • Questions you favorite
  • Custom decks you create
  • Spark Mode usage (question generation preferences)
  • Subscription status and tier
  • App usage analytics (anonymous)

Pandora Mode Data

  • Questions submitted during sessions
  • Session participation data
  • Note: All Pandora questions are automatically deleted after 24 hours for your privacy

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Connect app
  • To sync your favorites and custom decks across devices
  • To manage your subscription and billing
  • To generate AI questions through Spark Mode
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send important updates about the app (no spam!)
  • To provide customer support

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage
  • Cloud infrastructure: With enterprise-grade security
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data.

We only share data with:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For Spark Mode question generation (no personal identifiers sent)
  • Payment processors: For subscription billing (RevenueCat/Stripe)
  • Analytics providers: Anonymous usage data only
  • Legal authorities: Only if required by law

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@techykoala.com

Children's Privacy

Connect is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The "Tiny Talks" category is intended for children to use with parental supervision.

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Spark questions: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Pandora questions: Automatically deleted after session ends
  • Favorites & decks: Retained as long as your account exists
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 18 months
